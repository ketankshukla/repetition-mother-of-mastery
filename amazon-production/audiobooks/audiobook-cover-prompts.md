# Audiobook Cover Prompts - Repetition: Mother of Mastery Series

## Requirements
- **Dimensions:** 2400×2400px (square format)
- **Color Depth:** 24-bit color
- **Purpose:** Audiobook covers for ACX/Audible

---

## Book 1: The Foundation of Mastery

### Prompt for ChatGPT

Create a square audiobook cover (2400×2400px, 24-bit color) adapting the following book cover design from a tall portrait format to a perfect square:

**Central Figure:** A beautiful young woman in her 20s in a close-up three-quarter portrait, photorealistic with extraordinary detail — flawless yet natural skin, luminous eyes with visible iris detail, soft natural lips. She radiates simplicity and humility. Her eyes are closed in a moment of profound inner revelation, her expression carrying the wonder of someone who has just discovered the hidden architecture of mastery. Her lips part slightly as if absorbing a truth that changes everything. Lit with warm golden light.

**Background:** A luminous network of glowing golden neural pathways extends outward like a constellation behind her, symbolizing the brain rewiring itself through repetition. Deep midnight blue fading to black. Dramatic cinematic lighting from above creates a halo effect.

**Text Elements (preserve exact wording and hierarchy):**
- Top: "THE REPETITION SERIES" (small, elegant Garamond, gold)
- Below: "BOOK I" (Garamond, gold)
- Main Title: "THE FOUNDATION OF MASTERY" (large, elegant Garamond, white with subtle gold shimmer)
- Subtitle: "The Science and Psychology of Repetition" (smaller Garamond, properly spaced)
- Bottom: "KETAN SHUKLA" (large Garamond, generous letter spacing, white)

**Color Palette:** Deep midnight blue, gold, warm amber, white/cream text

**Adaptation Notes:** Maintain the vertical text hierarchy, keep the woman centered and prominent, neural pathways should fill the square composition naturally. Maintain the dramatic, ethereal, transformative mood.

## General Style Notes for All Covers

- **Photorealistic style** with cinematic lighting
- **Consistent visual identity** across all five books
- **Warm golden light** as the primary lighting source
- **Deep blue/black backgrounds** with golden accents
- **Garamond typography** throughout
- **The woman's face** should always be the focal point, filling at least 60% of the frame
- **Ethereal, aspirational mood** suggesting transformation and mastery
