Three overlapping golden circles form a Venn diagram, each representing a domain of mastery — sports (a running figure), arts (a paintbrush), and language (flowing script). Where all three circles overlap at the center, brilliant white light radiates outward. A beautiful young white woman in her 20s stands at the center intersection with arms outstretched, embodying mastery across all domains, with an expression of triumphant breakthrough. Deep midnight blue background with warm golden atmospheric glow. Photorealistic style with cinematic lighting. At the very top, "THE REPETITION SERIES" in small elegant Garamond font, centered, gold, letter-spacing 0.15em, font size 14pt. Directly below, "BOOK III" in Garamond font, centered, gold, letter-spacing 0.2em, font size 12pt. Below that, title "MASTERY IN ACTION" in large, elegant Garamond font, centered, gold. Subtitle "Sports, Arts, and Language" below in smaller Garamond, white. Author name "KETAN SHUKLA" at the bottom in large Garamond font, centered, generous letter spacing, gold.
