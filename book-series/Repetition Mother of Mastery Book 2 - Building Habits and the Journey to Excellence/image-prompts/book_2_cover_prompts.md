# Book 2 Cover Image Prompts

## Prompt 1: The Chain of Consistency

A stunning 9:16 portrait book cover. A beautiful young white woman in her 20s dominates the frame in a close-up three-quarter portrait, filling at least 70% of the image. Her face is photorealistic with extraordinary detail — flawless yet natural skin with subtle pores and light freckles, full defined eyebrows, luminous eyes with visible iris detail and natural catchlights, soft natural lips. She is stunningly gorgeous yet radiates simplicity and humility — there is no vanity in her beauty, only quiet strength and earned character. Her jaw is set with unshakable resolve, her eyes burn with the quiet fire of someone who has shown up every single day without exception, her lips carry the faintest trace of a knowing smile that says she understands what others do not: that consistency is the ultimate superpower. There is depth in her gaze — layers of early-morning discipline, of choosing the work when no one was watching, of building something invisible that is now undeniable. Her skin is lit with warm golden light, every contour and shadow rendered with cinematic precision. Behind her and slightly out of focus, an infinite chain of glowing golden links stretches vertically, each link representing a day of consistent practice. The chain glows with warm amber light against a deep navy blue background. Photorealistic style with dramatic cinematic lighting. At the very top, "THE REPETITION SERIES" in small elegant Garamond font, centered, gold, letter-spacing 0.15em, font size 14pt. Directly below, "BOOK II" in Garamond font, centered, gold, letter-spacing 0.2em, font size 12pt. Below that, title "THE HABIT ENGINE" in large, elegant Garamond font, centered, gold on dark background. Subtitle "Building Habits and the Journey to Excellence" below in smaller Garamond, white. Author name "KETAN SHUKLA" at the bottom in large Garamond font, centered, generous letter spacing, gold.
