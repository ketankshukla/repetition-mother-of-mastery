HEYGEN VIDEO PRODUCTION PROMPT
Book 1: The Foundation of Mastery — 3-Minute Marketing Video

=============================================================
OVERVIEW FOR HEYGEN
=============================================================

You are producing a 3-minute portrait-format marketing video for the book "The Foundation of Mastery" — Book 1 of the Repetition: Mother of Mastery series by Ketan Shukla.

You have been provided with the following assets:
- The book cover image for Book 1 (use as the opening and closing visual)
- 10 b-roll images labeled image-01 through image-10
- The author's logo

The video is portrait format (9:16 vertical), designed for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok. The presenter speaks directly to camera. B-roll images are displayed as full-screen background visuals that transition in sync with the script narration. Each image should appear when the narrator begins speaking about its corresponding concept and remain on screen for the duration of that passage. Transitions between images should be smooth cross-fades lasting approximately 0.5 seconds.

VIDEO SCRIPT WITH IMAGE CUE MARKERS
=============================================================

[OPENING — Display: BOOK COVER — duration: 8 seconds]
What if everything you have ever believed about talent is wrong?

[Transition to image-01 — The Talent Myth Shattering]
What if the people you admire most — the athletes, the musicians, the brilliant minds who seem to operate on another level — what if they did not start with some gift you were never given? What if the real secret behind every extraordinary skill ever developed by a human being comes down to one word? Repetition.

[Transition to image-02 — Neural Pathways Forming]
This is The Foundation of Mastery, Book One of the Repetition: Mother of Mastery series by Ketan Shukla. Inside these pages, you will discover exactly what happens inside your brain every single time you repeat a skill. You will learn how neural pathways physically form and strengthen with each repetition — how your brain literally rewires itself through a process called Hebbian learning, where neurons that fire together wire together.

[Transition to image-03 — Myelin Wrapping]
You will understand how a substance called myelin wraps around your nerve fibers every time you practice — making signals travel faster and with more precision, turning slow and clumsy movements into fast, fluid, effortless performance. This is not motivation. This is biology. This is the actual machinery of mastery happening inside your body every time you show up and do the work.

[Transition to image-04 — Neuroplasticity at Every Age]
And it goes even deeper. You will discover neuroplasticity — your brain's infinite capacity to grow and reorganize itself at any age. It does not matter if you are twenty or sixty. Your brain is built to learn, to adapt, to master new skills for as long as you are alive. The only question is whether you give it the repetitions it needs.

[Transition to image-05 — Deliberate Practice vs. Mindless Repetition]
But here is where this book separates itself from everything else you have read. It does not just tell you to practice more. It shows you the difference between deliberate practice and mindless repetition — and that difference is everything. Mindless repetition is going through the motions. Deliberate practice is focused, intentional, and targeted. This book lays out exactly how to practice deliberately so that every hour you invest delivers maximum results.

[Transition to image-06 — The Four Stages of Competence]
You will learn the four stages of competence — the predictable path that every human being walks from total ignorance to effortless mastery. You will understand exactly where you are on that path and what it takes to reach the next level.

[Transition to image-07 — The Spacing Effect]
You will discover the spacing effect — the scientific proof that distributing your practice over time produces dramatically better retention than cramming everything into marathon sessions.

[Transition to image-08 — Sleep and Memory Consolidation]
You will learn why sleep is not wasted time but one of the most powerful tools in your mastery arsenal — because while you sleep, your brain is actively consolidating and strengthening every pattern you practiced during the day.

[Transition to image-09 — Feedback Loops]
And you will master the art of feedback loops — the engine that transforms blind repetition into precision-guided growth, turning every practice session into a guaranteed step forward.

[Transition to image-10 — The Psychology of Persistence]
This book also confronts the psychology of persistence head on. The people who achieve mastery are not the ones who never wanted to quit. They are the ones who kept going anyway. This book shows you exactly how to develop persistence as a trainable skill — because that is precisely what it is.

[Transition back to BOOK COVER — duration: 15 seconds for closing]
The Foundation of Mastery is ten chapters of pure, evidence-based insight that will fundamentally rewire your understanding of human potential. After reading this book, you will never look at talent the same way again. You will see it for what it really is — the visible result of invisible repetitions, performed consistently, over time.

Get your copy of The Foundation of Mastery today on Amazon. And if you are ready to commit to the full transformation, pick up the complete Repetition: Mother of Mastery series. Five books. Fifty chapters. One unstoppable journey from where you are now to where you were always meant to be.

PRODUCTION NOTES FOR HEYGEN
=============================================================

- All image transitions: smooth cross-fade, 0.5 seconds
- Image display style: full-screen background, portrait crop (9:16), centered
- Presenter: on-camera, centered, speaking directly to viewer
- Presenter overlay: lower-third of frame when b-roll is displayed, or full-frame on book cover segments
- Text overlays (optional): key terms from the script can be displayed as animated lower-third captions in gold (#D4A843) Garamond font when the narrator speaks them — e.g., "MYELIN," "NEUROPLASTICITY," "DELIBERATE PRACTICE," "SPACING EFFECT," "FEEDBACK LOOPS"
- Music: warm, cinematic, instrumental — builds gradually from quiet and contemplative at the opening to powerful and inspiring at the closing CTA
- Music volume: low under narration (15-20%), full during opening 8 seconds and closing 15 seconds
- Pacing: measured and authoritative — not rushed. Allow each concept to land before transitioning.
